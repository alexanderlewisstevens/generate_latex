The history of video games began in the mid-20th century with simple computer-based games and evolved rapidly with the introduction of arcade machines, home consoles, and personal computers. Iconic games like Pong, Space Invaders, and Super Mario Bros. shaped the industry and influenced popular culture.

Major milestones include the rise of online multiplayer gaming, the development of 3D graphics, and the emergence of esports. The video game industry has become a significant part of global entertainment history.
